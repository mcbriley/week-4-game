Instructions

1. You will be given a random number at the start of each game.

2. By clicking on the four crystals below, you will add a specific amount of points to your score.

3. To win, match your score to the random number shown. If you exceed the random number, you lose.

4. The value of each crystal remains hidden from you, until you click on it.

5. Upon the start of a new game, the crystals will change values.

Good luck!
